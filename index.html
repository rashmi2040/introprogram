Healthy Mouth Happy Life

The Himalayan Dental Clinic and Training Center provides affordable and quality hands on training for new dental assistants and provide free, comprehensive dental treatment to our country’s most vulnerable people with disabilities or who are elderly or medically fragile two days in a week. These are people who cannot afford necessary treatment and cannot get public aid.

Why Choose Himalayan Dental Clinic

  • The hands on training will help to develop the confidence to do the work in the clinical settings.
  • Dental assistant training classes and treatments are affordable to everyone while it is free quality care for those in need two days in a week.
  • Highly Qualified Instructors
  • Great Clinical settings to learn
