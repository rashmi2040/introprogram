Our Staff

Dr. Shree Adhikari

Periodontist

Dr. Shree Adhikari strives to provide his patients with the most advanced periodontal and dental implant treatment. He received his Bachelor of Science from Southwest Baptist University in 2002. He is a 2006 graduate of Southern Illinois University School of Dental Medicine. Dr. Adhikari then completed a three year residency at St. Louis University and received a Master's of Science in Dentistry with a Certificate in Periodontology in 2009.

Susan Parker

RDH

Prior to her dental hygiene degree, Susan earned her BS in Biology from Kansas State University. Susan received her degree in Dental Hygiene from Saint Louis Community College in May 2015. Soon after graduation, Susan joined our team at Himalayan Dental Clinic and Training Center.

In her spare time, Susan enjoys being outdoors and attending sporting events. Susan enjoys working with parents and children in advancing good oral health practices. She enjoys working in an environment of like-minded peers and is encouraged by their positivity and kindness.

Tom Hanks

Dental Instructor

My name is Tom Hanks and I teach the Expanded Dental Assistant curriculum for Dental Office Training. I have 40 years of working experience in the field of dentistry. I attended J. Everett Light Career Center in 1974, where I received my Dental Assistant’s certificate. In 1980 I received my certification in Expanded Functions from I.U. School of Dentistry. I am currently working in the field and continue to look for ways to further my knowledge in every aspect of dentistry. Teaching has given me the ability to give back to the dental community here in Saint Louis, to help produce well-educated and trained dental assistants.
